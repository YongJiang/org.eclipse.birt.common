How to delete a row or column

1  
In the layout editor, hover the mouse pointer over the bottom left corner of the grid until the Grid tab appears, then choose the tab. Guide cells appear at the top and left side of the grid.
2  
Right-click the guide cell of the row or column to delete, then choose Delete from the context menu. If the row or column contains elements, the elements are also deleted.
